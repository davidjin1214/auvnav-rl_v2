Probe layouts considered for body-frame flow sensing on the AUV: (a) baseline DVL configuration (`s0`) with a single reference probe `p0` at the vehicle origin, (b) local cross-array configuration (`s1`) with four auxiliary probes placed at `(\pm 2, 0)` and `(0, \pm 2)` m around `p0`, and (c) forward-looking ADCP-inspired configuration (`s2`) with two axial probes at `(5, 0)` and `(9, 0)` m and two off-axis probes at `(8, \pm 4)` m. Dashed lines indicate the geometric arrangement of the auxiliary sensing locations relative to the reference probe. In all panels, coordinates are given in the AUV body frame, where `x_b` points forward and `y_b` points to port.
