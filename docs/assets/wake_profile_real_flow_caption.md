Figure X. Representative snapshots of the three wake-generation profiles used in `scripts/generate_wake.py`: (a) single-cylinder wake (`navigation`), (b) tandem dual-cylinder wake (`tandem_G35_nav`), and (c) side-by-side dual-cylinder wake (`side_by_side_G35_nav`). Background colors show vorticity `omega` from the generated wake data, and black streamlines indicate the corresponding velocity field `(u, v)`. Insets show the full simulation domain together with the ROI placement for each profile. The displayed frame in each panel is chosen automatically as the time index with the largest spatial RMS vorticity, so that a representative, well-developed wake pattern is shown. All panels share the same x-scale, while the y-range is cropped to the neighborhood of the cylinders and ROI to improve readability.
