Probe layouts considered for body-frame flow sensing on the AUV: (a) baseline DVL configuration (`s0`) with a single reference probe `p0` at the vehicle origin, (b) short-range forward ADCP configuration (`s1`) with one auxiliary probe at `(4.5, 0)` m ahead of `p0`, and (c) longer-range ADCP-inspired configuration (`s2`) with one axial probe at `(5, 0)` m and two off-axis probes at `(8, \pm 4)` m in addition to `p0`. Dashed lines indicate the geometric arrangement of the sensing locations relative to the reference probe. In all panels, coordinates are given in the AUV body frame, where `x_b` points forward and `y_b` points to port.
